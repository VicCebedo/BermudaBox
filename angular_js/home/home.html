BermudaBox

Exploring the idea of using Time-based One-time Passwords (TOTP) , typically used in 2FA (Two-Factor Authentication) via Google Authenticator , as an alternative protection against Cross-Site Request Forgery (CSRF) and automated attacks (usually protected by CAPTCHA ). This project also explores the idea of using TOTP as an alternative Stateless authentication method.
